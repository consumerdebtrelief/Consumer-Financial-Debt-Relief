Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our debt relief programs

How much can I save with your debt relief program?

Our clients typically save between 30-50% of their enrolled debt before fees. The exact amount depends on your specific financial situation, types of debt, and creditor policies. During your free consultation, we'll provide a personalized estimate of potential savings.

Will debt relief affect my credit score?

Initially, your credit score may be impacted as we negotiate with creditors. However, as you make consistent payments and reduce your debt, most clients see significant improvement in their credit scores within 12-24 months. Our program is designed to help you rebuild your credit over time.

How long does the debt relief process take?

Programs typically last between 24-48 months, depending on your total debt amount, ability to make monthly payments, and creditor negotiation timelines. We create a customized plan during your consultation that outlines the expected timeline for your specific situation.

Are there upfront fees for your services?

No, there are absolutely no upfront fees. We only charge fees after we successfully negotiate and settle your debts. Our fees are a percentage of the debt savings we achieve for you, so our success is directly tied to your success.

What types of debt do you work with?

We specialize in unsecured debts including credit cards, personal loans, medical bills, private student loans, and certain business debts. We do not work with secured debts (like mortgages or auto loans) or federal student loans.
